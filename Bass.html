About Us

Bugema Adventist Secondary School provides a nurturing environment for students in grades one through six. Dedicated teachers guide students on a rigorous academic journey, fostering critical thinking and a love of learning. Beyond the classroom, a vibrant extracurricular program allows students to explore their talents in sports, arts, and clubs. Bugema Adventist Secondary School prepares its graduates for success in college and beyond, instilling in them a strong sense of community, responsibility, and a lifelong pursuit of knowledge.
